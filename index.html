NodeJS

EJS

Javascript

CSS

HTML

Bad sleep can wreak havoc on life. A solution provided by neurobiologist Andrew Huberman is getting sunlight in your eyes when waking up because it regulates your circadian rhythm. Having normal bedtime and wake times is one of the most effective ways to get better sleep. Light Rizer uses OpenWeatherMap and Twilio API to send you a reminder text when the sun has risen in your area, so you can get out and get some light.
